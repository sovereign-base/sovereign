  - DATABASE bottlenecks — connection pooling, indexing, and the TRIGGERS for sharding/partitioning

    tells you which.

  trade-off) are OFFERED to `/adr-log` — point the user to it or describe the decision for it.
  scale-design does NOT number or write ADRs itself; adr-log is the single owner of ADR recording.

  (that's `deploy-design` / construction, DEFERRED to Phase 9 / later).
