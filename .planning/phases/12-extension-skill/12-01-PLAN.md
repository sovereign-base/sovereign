<objective>
Correct `engine/bin/lib/extension.cjs` to the VERIFIED `npx skills` surface discovered by the Phase-12 live smoke-test, and update its test suite to match. The Phase-10 module was built on wrong `skills` flags: `preview` passed `-a <agent> --copy` to `skills use` (but `use` rejects `--copy` and treats `-a` as "start that agent INTERACTIVELY"), and `audit` assumed `skills use` materialized a file/dir on disk to read. The real surface: **bare `skills use <source>` prints the prompt-wrapped raw SKILL.md to STDOUT** — that stdout IS the auditable content.

Purpose: Without this correction the extension skill (12-02) would wrap a broken engine — preview would hang/error and audit would scan an empty path. The skill must drive on the engine's `scanSkillContent` findings (EXT-02), so the engine's audit must actually produce them from real `skills use` output.

Output: A corrected `extension.cjs` (preview = bare use; audit takes a source, runs use, scans stdout) and an updated `extension.test.cjs`, with full `node --test` green and zero-dep/array-args/output() invariants held.
</objective>
